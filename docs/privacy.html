Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 July 2025  ·  InnerType for iOS

The short version

  • Everything you enter stays on your device. Nothing is sent to any server.
  • InnerType has no account system. We cannot identify you.
  • We collect no analytics, no crash reports, no usage data.
  • We serve no ads and never will.
  • The only payment data involved in a purchase is handled entirely by Apple.

1. What we collect

Nothing. InnerType does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data on any server we operate or control.

All data generated by the app — your name (if you choose to enter one), assessment answers, results, profile scores, and settings — is stored exclusively in your device's local storage using Apple's standard AsyncStorage mechanism. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly share something using your device's own share sheet.

2. No account, no identity

InnerType requires no account, no login, and no email address. We have no mechanism to identify who you are, and we do not want one. You are anonymous to us by design.

3. Analytics and crash reporting

We do not use any analytics SDK, crash reporting tool, or event-tracking library. We have no visibility into how you use the app, how often you open it, or what features you use.

Apple may collect aggregated, anonymized App Store metrics (such as download counts and crash rate summaries) through standard developer dashboards. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours, and cannot be traced back to individual users.

4. In-app purchases

If you choose to unlock InnerType Premium, the purchase is processed entirely by Apple through StoreKit. We do not see, store, or handle your payment information. The only confirmation we receive from Apple is whether the purchase was completed — no financial details are shared with us.

Your payment data is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Sharing your results

InnerType includes an optional feature to share your assessment result as an image or text. If you use it:

  • On iOS, sharing uses the native iOS share sheet. The app does not send anything to our servers.
  • Where you share (a messaging app, social platform, etc.) is your choice. That destination's own privacy policy governs what happens once you share.

Sharing is always optional and always initiated by you.

6. Third-party services

InnerType does not integrate with any third-party advertising networks, data brokers, or social login providers. The app contains no trackers.

The only third-party involvement is Apple's App Store infrastructure for distribution and payment processing, and Apple's standard iOS frameworks used by the app. These are governed by Apple's policies.

7. Children's privacy

InnerType is not directed at children under 13. Because we collect no personal data from anyone, we have no data about children to manage. If you are a parent or guardian with concerns, please contact us.

8. Data deletion

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you control it entirely. To delete everything:

  • Open InnerType → Settings → Reset all data. This clears all stored data from your device immediately and permanently.
  • Alternatively, uninstalling the app removes all local data.

There is no data on our side to request deletion of, because we never received any.

9. Changes to this policy

If we ever introduce features that involve data collection, this policy will be updated and the effective date changed. We will provide notice within the app before any material change takes effect. The current version will always be available at this URL.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach us at privacy@innertype.app.

We are a small, independent team. We read every message.
